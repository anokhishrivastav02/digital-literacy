# Stay Safe Online Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN with anyone.
2. Do not approve unknown payment requests.
3. Verify the identity of buyers and sellers online.
4. Avoid clicking suspicious links in messages or emails.
5. Enable two-factor authentication on accounts.
6. Regularly update passwords and apps.
7. Do not share OTPs with anyone.
8. Use secure networks when making online payments.

If you experience cyber fraud:
Report at https://cybercrime.gov.in
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
